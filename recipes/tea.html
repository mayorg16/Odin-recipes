TEA RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

Tea de canela, mexican tea

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of ceylon cinnamon
  • choice of sweetener such as honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice

STEPS

  1. place cinnamon sticks in a pot and boil
  2. strain tea and poyr into a cup
  3. add lemon juice and sweetener
