GOULASH RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

This is the american goulash recipe end product

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 large onion,diced
  • 2 large bay leaves

STEPS

  1. pour two glasses of water into the pot
  2. leave it to boil
  3. pour the bay leaves into the boiling water
  4. mix the ingredients
  5. voila you have the goulash meal
